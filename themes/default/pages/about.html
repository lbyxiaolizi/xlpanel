About OpenHost

We're building the future of hosting management — open, transparent, and community-driven.

Our Mission

OpenHost was born out of frustration with the existing hosting management landscape. Proprietary solutions are expensive, inflexible, and often leave hosting providers locked into ecosystems that don't serve their best interests.

We believe that critical business infrastructure should be open source. That's why we created OpenHost — a powerful, flexible, and completely open alternative that gives hosting providers full control over their business.

Open Source • Global • Community
